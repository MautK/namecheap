Pop-Up Energy

A design proposal for vacant plots of land in the harbour area of Amsterdam

Combining functional with aesthetics, in this design an unique energy landscape experience is created. Focussing on eye-catchers of the harbour, seasonality and suspense, a route is created that turns this unused land into a new event.
