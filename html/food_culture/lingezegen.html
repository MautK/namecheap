Sustainable Food Cultures

In collaboration with Ivar Boerma, Jelmer Boersma, Nora Kooijmans and professor Gabrielle Bartelse

A design for a sustainable food forest in which permaculture principles are combined with landscape experiences.

With this design we want to show that food production can also have a aesthetical and leisure value to it, without losing the production qualities.
