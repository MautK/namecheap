I could have been at the beach right now

Following the performative walk, along the topographical border between high- and low-Netherlands, derives this immersive installation. While the screens display the landscapes and the heroic engagement with the landscapes, an hacked laser and fog machine contribute to wave-simulations. In doing so, the installation both represents the physical components of the walk and the constructed image that I developed during the walk.
