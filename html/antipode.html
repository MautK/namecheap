Antipodes

Ever wondered what the other side of the world would look like?

On this website you get the chance to explore the direct other side of the world. As if you would dig a tunnel through the middle of the earth and stick your head out at the other end. After entering your current location the program brings you to a video of google Streetview images edited together into a 360 degrees spin. As if you were turning your head around at that other side of the tunnel.

In progress - currently working on giving the right feedback for if you end up in a place where Google has not yet come.
