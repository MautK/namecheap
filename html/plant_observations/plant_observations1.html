plant observations

More and more the question arrises what our human connection to the world around us is. Can we control it? Should we control? In what way is control desirable?

Coming from the Netherland the question of what natural and what nature management is, is very actual. That is why for once I just wanted to start with plain observations. Observe how seeds are reacting to their surroundings in the process of growth.

For several days I was drawing the current state in order to register their progress. Funny, because in the end they look like real personalities to me.
